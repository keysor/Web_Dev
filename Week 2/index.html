Baldur's Gate 3 is a very fun 1-4 CO-OP player game. It features a lot of role playing and content where the player can choose multiple outcomes for every situation changing how the game is played with every playthrough. I enjoy the replayability with friends and on my solo adventures. It's not fast paced and you can choose how the game is played. Anything you can think about doing, you probably can in this game.
